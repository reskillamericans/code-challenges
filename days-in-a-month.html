How Many Days in the Month?

A common problem in working with dates, is to calculate the days of the month for any given month and year. Due to leap years, the rules can get a little tricky. Here are the rules in English:

  • Jan, Mar, May, Jul, Aug, Oct, Dec have 31 days.
  • Apr, Jun, Sep, Nov have 30 days.
  • Feb has 28 days EXCEPT when it is a leap year, and then it has 29.
  • Leap Year Rules
    • If the year is divisible by 4, it's a leap year.
    • EXCEPT, if it's divisible by 100, then it's NOT a leap year.
    • EXCEPT, if it's divisible by 400, then it really is a leap year (again).

So, leap years include 2000 and 2004, but NOT 1900 or 2100.
